24 April 2020

AustationBot updated:

  • nerfed flashes
  • Contractor baton knockdown causes item drop again.
  • Non-flashproof sunglasses have been added to the ClothesMate.
  • All flashproof sunglasses are now named "advanced sunglasses" and subtypes have their descriptions changed.
  • Meta and Delta static maint sunglasses have been swapped with dynamic loot spawns.
  • felinids are weak to loud noises now, not just to ear damage
  • Nanotrasen has provided the on-board botanists with their own minor botanical chemical dispenser for advanced botanical duties.
  • makes flash blindness true blindness for half of the duration
  • fixes borg flash blindness
  • Added flashbulbs, the thing that powers the flash.
  • Added flashbulbs to basic security tech, and they can be printed from a security protolathe.
  • Flash now has a 20 decisecond forced cooldown (2 seconds)
  • Removes confusion from flashes completely.
  • Basic security research now unlocks flashbulbs.
  • Flashbulbs can be removed from flashes using wirecutters and inserted if burnt out.
  • Flash boxes now spawn with 2 flashes and 4 spare security bulbs.
  • Flash burnout is now based on charges rather than how fast you use it and luck.
  • Flash AOE no longer stuns and no longer confuses people.
  • Exosuit fabricator flashes now only have 4 charges. icon: Adds in new icons for flashbulbs.
  • added a verb to display a humorous video in the case of a bug
  • Classic EMP grenades now break lights also.
  • replaced the normal sunglasses in the detectives offices with a sechud
  • Various helmets now have the same stats as their counterpart suit's stats
  • Flagellant hood (the head part) now has 0 armor instead of negative
  • CE hardsuit now has 100 rad protection
  • Energy chainsaws no longer revert to normal chainsaws when implanted
  • disposals loops are no longer inescapable if you dont have explosives
  • Added more botany spawn flags to box and pubby.
  • Flypeople now get a viable amount of nutrients from vomit. No more starving.
  • Gaseous decomposition now has capital letters
  • decreased reagent cap on carbon mobs to 1000
  • Changeling IPCs will now use the backup name
  • zombies and skeletons can metabolize chems again
  • Flypeople can now vomit even if they're starving.
  • Flypeople will only vomit when consuming food.
